    A brief history about us

    Lakeside's math team began in the 2000-2001 school year with a small group of about 20 third graders. Since then, the math team has grown to include nearly 200 3rd, 4th, and 5th grade students every year. Each school year, math teamers practice for and then compete in three official contests through the Online Math League.
